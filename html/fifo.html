1. Export your Exchange or wallet data.

2. Open or paste it into Excel or Google Sheets.

3. Trim your data into two columns only, Date and Token Amount. Price per token is optional third column.

4. Sort the column by token amount to separate the buys and sells using +/-

5. Move all the buys into one column with dates and all sells into another column or tab with dates. Columns will be of different lengths.

6. Paste tab separated values from Excel below. (+/- sign doesn't matter as long as they are all the same sign)
